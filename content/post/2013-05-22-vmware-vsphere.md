Title: How to active vmware vSphere 5.1 by free
Date: 2013-05-22

1) go to http://vmware.com/go/get-free-esxi, register an account, and apply
for an free licence.
2) Open vsphere->configuration->License Feature->Edit->Assign a new license
key to this host
